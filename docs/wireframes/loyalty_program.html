Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the Greenway Rewards Program?

You can join our rewards program by signing up online through our website or in-store at any Greenway Marijuana location. You'll need to provide basic information like your name, email, phone number, and verify that you're 21 or older with a valid ID.

How do I earn points?

You earn points based on your purchases at Greenway Marijuana. For every $1 spent on qualifying purchases, you earn 1 point. Gold tier members earn 1.25 points per dollar, and Platinum tier members earn 1.5 points per dollar. Points are automatically added to your account after each purchase.

Do my points expire?

Points never expire as long as your account remains active. An account is considered active if you make at least one purchase within a 12-month period. If your account is inactive for more than 12 months, points may expire.

How do I redeem my rewards?

You can redeem your rewards in-store or online during checkout. Simply let our budtender know you'd like to use your points, or select the option to apply rewards during online checkout. You can choose to redeem points for discounts, free products, or exclusive merchandise.

How do I check my points balance?

You can check your points balance by logging into your account on our website, asking a budtender in-store, or calling our customer service line. Your current points balance is also displayed on your receipt after each purchase.

Join the Greenway Rewards Program today and start earning points with your very first purchase. It's free to join and the benefits are immediate!
