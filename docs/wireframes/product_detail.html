About Blue Dream

Blue Dream is one of the most popular strains in the United States. Beginning its life in California, this sativa-dominant hybrid is a cross between Blueberry and Haze, creating a pleasant blend of effects. Blue Dream is relaxing and soothing but not sleepy, and it can be great for pain relief.

This strain is ideal for daytime use and can be suitable for both beginners and experienced users. The high is known to come on slowly but then provide a nice balance between cerebral stimulation and gentle body relaxation.

Blue Dream features a delicious flavor profile that combines sweet berry notes inherited from its Blueberry parent with subtle herbal and spicy undertones from Haze. The aroma is similarly pleasant, with hints of blueberry, earth, and a touch of citrus.

Medical marijuana patients often choose Blue Dream to help relieve symptoms associated with depression, chronic pain, and nausea. Its balanced effects make it versatile for various conditions without being too overwhelming.
